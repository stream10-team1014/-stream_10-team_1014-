Cybersecurity Opportunities




Industry Opportunities

The global cybersecurity market is rapidly growing. It is estimated to be worth 639 billion US dollars by 2023.
It is predicted that data analytics, mobile internet, cloud and internet of things will also grow hugely,
generating hundreds of billions in sales per year. The cybersecurity sectotr is responsible for employing hundreds of thousands of people, and job opportunities continue to grow.
Cybersecurity jobs were the sixth most advertised ICT occupation on linkedin in 2015.

As society , business and individuals are moving towards total reliance on the internet, especially in a post-covid world, the threat and damages of cyberattacks will only grow.
Businesses will by necessity need to focus more time and money on security, as the duties and responsibilities regarding cybersecurity may move onto businesses and governments
in order to protect customers/citizens. Even businesses who previously felt they did not need to pay any attention to cybersecurity will by necessity have to make significant
choices. Smart infrastructure, Internet of Things/Internet of Everything, Big Data / Cloud computing, Wearable Tech, Integrated ICT Systems in Personal Health and Government
records and communications, all point to a move towards a necessity to consider cybersecurity much more seriously.

Government Opportunities

Governments could focus more money and resources toward the problem. Potential areas of focus include :

  • Creating security standards
  • Education system safeguards including new qualifications and certifications specific to cybersecurity
  • Legally defining cybersecurity laws and crimes and increasing penalties and powers to prosecute/investigate
  • Legal rules and requirements for goverment compliance by private companies and individuals
  • Allocating more funding to universities
  • Adopting official security vendors
  • Creating / increasing funding for specialist cybersecurity in government sector


Individual Opportunities

What does this mean for individuals? Due to the increasing severity of the problem and the required increase in cybersecurity spending and growth of the cybersecurity sector , individuals will have many opportunities to work in this sector. More and more people may begin to work and study in this area out of necessity of a larger workforce and/or increased govermental attention to the issue.

Cybersecurity Choices




Government choices

Choices available to governments include:

  • Address shortages of highly-skilled cybersecurity professionals by encouraging students to study cybersecurity or related courses
  • Promoting awareness among citizens
  • Participating in international cybersecurity efforts
  • Defining digital human rights, and clearly define laws pertaining to all cybercrimes
  • Increasing police cybersecurity funding and actions
  • Developing more specific cybersecurity laws
  • Increasing univerisity/polytechnic funding for the purposes of training more security professionals
  • Establishing national cybersecurity agencies
  • Establishing national critical infrastructure protection programs
  • Establishing national incident response and recovery plans


Business choices

Choices available to businesses include:

  • Deploying Unified threat management systems
  • Using a spam filter
  • Using antivirus software
  • Using a patch management system
  • Enabling 2-factor authentication
  • Encrypting data
  • Backing up data regularly
  • Filtering content to prevent access to high risk websites
  • Having a disaster recovery plan


Individual choices

Choices available to individuals include:

  • Practicing good password selection
  • Keeping your Wi-Fi secure
  • Installing antivirus software
  • Avoiding Suspicious Emails and Texts
  • Using Firewalls and Encryption
  • Physically securing mobile devices. (Avoiding theft or loss)
  • Practicing good bluetooth and GPS usage
  • Taking courses related to cybersecurity
